## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I change plans?
Yes, upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes take effect at the next billing cycle.

### What happens if I exceed my candidate limit?
You'll receive a notification at 80% capacity. You can upgrade or archive old candidates.

### Do you charge per user?
No. All plans include unlimited users.

### Is there a setup fee?
No setup fees on any plan. Onboarding packages are optional.

### Can I cancel anytime?
Yes on monthly plans (30-day notice). Annual plans require 90-day notice after first year.

### Do you offer non-profit discounts?
Yes, 25% off any plan for registered 501(c)(3) organizations.

### What payment methods do you accept?
Credit card (Visa, MC, Amex), ACH transfer, wire transfer, PayPal (annual plans).

### Is training included?
Documentation and videos are included. Live training is included in Professional+ plans.

### Can I get a custom quote?
Yes, contact sales for custom requirements or volume pricing.
